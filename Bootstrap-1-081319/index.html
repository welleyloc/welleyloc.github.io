Kimchi Fried Rice

Do you have too much rice in the fridge? Got 30 minutes to spare, but don't want to spend more than 40? Well, you're looking at the right thing! This is going to be the best fried rice ever!

Serving size: ♨ ♨ ♨ ♨

Difficulty: 1/10

Calories per serving: 300/2000

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 2.5 cups of leftover rice (cooked)
  • 1/4 cup fresh/frozen corn
  • 1 stalk of chopped spring onion
  • 1/4 cup diced kimchi
  • 1/2 diced yellow onion
  • 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • a pinch of ground pepper

Directions

  1. Add the raw eggs to the rice and break up any chunks to loosen the grains. Mix thoroughly.
  2. Heat up oil in a pan and sautee the onions with kimchi until translucent.
  3. Add the rice to the pan and stir fry until it is evenly coated in kimchi juice.
  4. Add the sauces to the mix and sear the rice until a little scorched.
  5. Lightly toss in the spring onions and add pepper to taste.
